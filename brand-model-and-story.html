Mission.
What We Do

ComPsych delivers human-centered support that flows naturally between life and work, covering everything from mental and physical health to legal, organizational, and absence support.

We provide real help for real people, in every moment, whether they are thriving at work or facing time away.

Our mission is to create workplaces that do more than just function. We aim to build environments that truly lift people up.

We partner with organizations that believe people support drives business success, turning challenges into strength for individuals, teams, and the entire company.

Subscript

With a clinical background and an empathic vision, they guide organizations and individuals through crisis and toward better days. They turn complex solutions into deeply personal support, answering the phone at 3 a.m. with the understanding that it could save a life.

For over 40 years, they have combined wisdom and wonder, speaking with the confidence gained from helping millions, while listening as if each person is the only one. They build networks of support that stretch across boardrooms and time zones, lifting people when they are ready to rise.

The Nurturing Guide believes that within every human struggle, there is strength. In every moment of vulnerability, there is a chance for transformation. This is what drives themnot simply to help, but to awaken resilience.

Brand Story

Brand Story

ComPsychs story is not just about what we offer. It is about who we serve and why we exist.

Over 40 years ago, we started with a simple but powerful belief: when people are truly supported, they can overcome anything.That belief led us to create something unique and lasting. A support system as wide as human needs. One that helps people navigate mental health, workplace challenges, life events, and everything in between.

We have never viewed work and life as separate. A crisis at home can impact the workplace. An absence from work often carries a personal story. That is why our model connects every part of the human experience. Whether someone is doing well, facing difficulty, or somewhere in between.

Today, ComPsych is the largest provider of integrated employee support in the world. But at our core, we are still a people-first partner. We are present in over 200 countries. We listen with empathy, respond with expertise, and help people build resilience wherever they are.

We do not just support the workforce. We support the human force behind it.
